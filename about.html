ABOUT
Ali Tabatabaie Ghomi is a designer and architect with expertise in computational and digital design and fabrication. He holds a Master's of Science in Design from the University of Pennsylvania. Along with years of experience as a professional architectural designer, he is also actively working in other fields including furniture design, product design, experimental art, etc. His design methodology is focused on the quality of experience that the design creates for its audience. He tries to create unique experiences and aesthetics with the help of technology and the skillful use of the latest design tools and methods. Nature is a major influencer and inspiration in his works.
